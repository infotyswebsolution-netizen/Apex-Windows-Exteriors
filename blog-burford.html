Burford & Mount Pleasant: Eavestrough Care Secrets

By Apex Expert Team | March 12, 2026

For homeowners in Burford and Mount Pleasant, the beautiful mature tree cover can be a double-edged sword. While providing shade, those same trees can quickly clog your eavestroughs, leading to serious foundation risks.

1. The Hidden Dangers of Clogs

When gutters overflow, water pools at the base of your foundation. Over time, this saturates the soil and puts immense pressure on your basement walls, leading to cracks, leaks, and costly repairs. Regular cleaning in Burford is not just about looks—it's structural insurance.

2. Micro-Mesh Gutter Guards: The Permanent Solution

If you're tired of climbing ladders twice a year, micro-mesh gutter guards are the answer. These allow water to flow freely while blocking leaves, pine needles, and even asphalt shingle grit. It's a one-time investment that protects your Mount Pleasant property for life.
