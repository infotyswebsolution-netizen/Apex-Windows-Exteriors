# Required DNS Records for Apex Windows & Exteriors

To complete Priority 5 (Email / Domain Trust) of the SEOptimer audit, the following DNS records must be added to your domain registrar (e.g., Vercel, GoDaddy, or Namecheap).

## 1. SPF Record (Sender Policy Framework)
Prevents email spoofing by authorizing specific IP addresses/services to send mail from your domain.

- **Type**: TXT
- **Host**: @ (or leave blank)
- **Value**: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all
*(Assuming you use Google Workspace. If you use a different provider, please let me know for an updated record.)*

## 2. DMARC Record (Domain-based Message Authentication)
Tells email servers how to handle messages that fail SPF or DKIM checks.

- **Type**: TXT
- **Host**: _dmarc
- **Value**: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:info@apexwindowsexteriors.com

## 3. DKIM Record (Recommended)
You should also enable DKIM in your email provider's admin panel (e.g., Google Workspace Admin) and add the resulting TXT record to your DNS to ensure maximum deliverability.
