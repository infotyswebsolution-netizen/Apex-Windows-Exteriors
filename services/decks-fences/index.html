FAQ

Decks & Fences Questions

Do I need a permit for my deck?

In Hamilton and Grimsby, most decks higher than 24 inches or larger than 108 sq.ft require a building permit. We handle the entire application and inspection process for you.

How long does it take to build a fence?

For a standard residential lot, post setting typically takes one day, followed by 1-2 days for framing and board installation. Total time varies by lot size and soil conditions.

What is the best material for a low-maintenance deck?

Composite decking is the best choice for busy homeowners. While the initial investment is higher, you save thousands in the long run on staining, sanding, and sealing costs.

Can you build over an existing concrete pad?

Yes! We can often install a "sleeper system" or use specific structural brackets to build your new deck directly over old concrete, saving you the cost of demolition.
