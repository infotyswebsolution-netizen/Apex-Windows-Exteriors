DECKS & FENCES FAQ

What is the best material for a new deck?

It depends on your budget and maintenance preference. Composite decking (like Trex) is virtually maintenance-free but higher cost. Pressure-treated wood is the most affordable, while Cedar offers a natural, premium look with moderate maintenance.

How deep do fence posts need to be in Ontario?

In Southern Ontario (Grimsby/Hamilton), fence posts should be buried at least 4 feet deep to get below the frost line. This prevents "frost heave" from pushing your fence out of the ground during winter.

Do I need a permit for a new deck?

Permit requirements vary by municipality (Grimsby, Hamilton, etc.) and the height/size of the deck. Generally, decks over 2 feet above grade require a permit. Apex can help you navigate the local building codes.
