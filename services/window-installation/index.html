WINDOW TYPES

Find the Perfect Fit for Your Home

Every home has unique architectural needs. We provide a massive selection of high-performance vinyl windows designed for Ontario's extreme temperature swings.

🏠 Casement Windows

Best for maximum ventilation and security. These hinged windows swing outward like a door and offer the tightest seal against drafts.

🪟 Double-Hung Windows

Classic aesthetic with modern functionality. Both sashes slide vertically, allowing for easy cleaning from inside the home.

🖼️ Bay & Bow Windows

Expand your interior space and let in natural light. Perfect for living rooms looking to create a scenic focal point.

☀️ Picture Windows

Large, fixed-pane windows that offer unobstructed views. Because they don't open, they provide the highest possible energy efficiency.

FAQ

Window Installation Questions

How much do new windows cost in Ontario?

Costs vary based on style and size, but our "Best Price Guarantee" ensures we beat any written estimate in Grimsby, Hamilton, and Burlington. Call us for a fast, accurate quote.

Can you install windows in the winter?

Yes! We use specialized winter installation techniques to replace windows one at a time, minimizing heat loss in your home. We install windows across Niagara and Hamilton year-round.

How long does an installation take?

A standard full-house replacement usually takes 1 to 2 days. Single window installs take just a few hours. We prioritize fast, professional turnovers.

Are vinyl windows better than wood or aluminum?

For Ontario's climate, vinyl is the top choice for price and performance. Unlike wood, they won't rot or peel, and unlike aluminum, they have extremely high thermal resistance.
