SOFFIT & FASCIA FAQ

Why is soffit ventilation important?

Properly vented soffit allows fresh air to enter your attic, pushing out hot, moist air through your roof vents. This prevents "attic rain" (internal condensation), reduces cooling costs in summer, and prevents ice dams in winter.

Do I need to paint my new aluminum fascia?

No! Our aluminum fascia systems come with a factory-baked finish that is designed to last for decades without peeling, cracking, or fading. It is virtually maintenance-free.

How do I know if my fascia needs replacing?

Look for signs of peeling paint, soft spots in the wood behind the aluminum, or gutters that are pulling away from the house. These are signs that moisture has penetrated the system and replacement is needed.
