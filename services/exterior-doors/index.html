MATERIAL OPTIONS

Steel vs. Fiberglass: Built for Ontario Winters

We exclusively install doors engineered to survive Canada's climate extremes, meaning zero warping, cracking, or severe expansion.

High-Security Steel Doors

The ultimate choice for structural strength and security. Steel doors offer smooth finishes perfectly suited for modern aesthetics and boast massive insulation cores.

Premium Fiberglass Doors

The gold standard for durability and beauty. Fiberglass resists denting and scratching, requires virtually no maintenance, and can be stained to mimic the exact texture of rich mahogany or oak wood.

THE APEX ADVANTAGE

Our 3-Step Installation Process

1. Precise Measurement

Every home shifts over time. Our experts measure your doorframe down to the millimeter to ensure an airtight, perfectly level custom fit before manufacturing begins.

2. Frame Optimization

We don't just hang a door. We inspect the underlying rough opening, repair any wood rot, and inject high-density insulating foam deep into the frame gaps.

3. Same-Day Setup

Your home will never be left exposed. Our removal and installation process happens entirely in a single day, followed by a meticulous clean-up and hardware testing.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Exterior Door FAQs

How much does a new front door installation cost?

Costs vary based on material (steel vs. fiberglass), glass inserts, framing needs, and hardware. Our basic steel entry doors are highly affordable, while custom fiberglass configurations represent a premium investment. Contact us for a free, exact quote.

Are new exterior doors energy efficient?

Absolutely. Modern exterior doors are injected with dense polyurethane foam cores, vastly outperforming old wood doors. Coupled with our professional weather-stripping installation, you will immediately notice pitch-perfect draft reduction.

Do you service my area?

Yes, our primary service footprint covers Grimsby, Hamilton, Burlington, Brantford, and the broader Niagara Region.
