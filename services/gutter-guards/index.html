PROTECTION BENEFITS

Why Every Ontario Home Needs Gutter Guards

Living in the Niagara and Hamilton region means dealing with heavy autumn leaves and intense winter ice. Gutter guards are not just a luxury; they are a critical insurance policy for your home's structural integrity.

🏗️
Foundation Protection: Clogged gutters overflow, pooling water at your foundation which leads to basement leaks and structural cracks.
🏠
Prevent Ice Dams: By keeping gutters clear of wet debris, our guards help reduce the formation of dangerous ice dams in the winter.
🪜
Ladder Safety: Gutter cleaning is one of the leading causes of home accidents. Eliminate the need to climb forever.
🦟
Pest Control: Stagnant water in clogged gutters is a breeding ground for mosquitoes and can attract rodents and birds.

QUESTIONS?

Gutter Guard FAQs

Will seeds and pine needles get through the mesh?

No. Our micro-mesh gaps are smaller than a pinpoint. Even shingle grit, pine needles, and maple seeds (helicopters) cannot penetrate the guard, ensuring only water enters your system.

Can the guards handle heavy rainfall?

Yes. Our systems are rated to handle over 10 inches of rain per hour—far exceeding any storm record in Ontario's history. The water is pulled through the mesh while debris slides off the top.

Do you clean the gutters before installation?

Absolutely. Our full service includes a professional cleaning of your entire gutter system, downspout flushing, and minor repairs/sealing before the guards are permanently secured.

How long do gutter guards last?

Because we use stainless steel and aluminum instead of PVC, our guards are designed to last for the lifetime of your gutters—often 20 to 30 years or more.
