COMMON ISSUES

Siding Problems We Solve Daily

Don't let a small crack turn into a major structural bill. Our team handles everything from minor cosmetic fixes to complex moisture barrier repairs.

🔨 Wind & Impact Damage

High winds in Grimsby can rattle panels loose or cause impact cracks. We secure loose siding and replace shattered panels instantly.

🔥 Heat Distortion

Vinyl siding can melt or warp if a BBQ or reflected sunlight from energy-efficient windows gets too hot. We can remove and replace warped sections with ease.

💧 Moisture Underlayment

If water gets behind your siding, it can rot your wood frame. We don't just fix the skin; we ensure your water barrier is intact.

WHY REPAIR NOW?

FAQ

Siding Repair Questions

How much does a siding repair cost?

Minor repairs like a single panel replacement or trim fix start at $199. Larger storm damage repairs depend on the linear footage. We provide clear, fixed estimates.

Can you match the color of my old siding?

Yes. While vinyl can fade slightly over 15+ years, we have access to hundreds of profiles and colors. We can often pull a panel from a less visible area of the house to use for a perfect front-facing match.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes! If your siding was damaged by hail or a major wind storm, we can provide the detailed quotes and documentation your insurance adjuster needs.
