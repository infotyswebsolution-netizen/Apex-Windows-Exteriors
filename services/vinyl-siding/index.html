FAQ

Vinyl Siding Questions

How long does vinyl siding last?

High-quality vinyl siding is designed to last 30 to 40 years. Our premium options come with fade-resistant protection and lifetime warranties to ensure long-term curb appeal.

What is the best siding for Grimsby & Hamilton?

Because of our local lakeshore winds and moisture, insulated vinyl siding is often the best choice for balancing thermal protection, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness.

Can vinyl siding be repaired after storm damage?

Yes! We offer a dedicated siding repair service for Grimsby and Hamilton. Starting at $199, we can replace individual panels or trim to restore your home's integrity.

How do I clean my new siding?

Vinyl is incredibly low maintenance. A simple annual wash with a garden hose and a soft brush/mild soap is usually all that's required to keep it looking new for decades.
