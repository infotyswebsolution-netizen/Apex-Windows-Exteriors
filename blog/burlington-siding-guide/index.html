Burlington Homeowners Guide to Vinyl Siding in 2026

By Apex Windows & Exteriors  |  April 3, 2026  |  Burlington

Why Burlington Homes Are Upgrading Their Siding in 2026

Burlington sits right on Lake Ontario, making homes especially vulnerable to freeze-thaw cycles, salt air, and intense UV exposure from long summer days. Older wood, stucco, or aluminum siding absorbs moisture and slowly deteriorates — costing homeowners thousands in rot repair and repainting.

Premium vinyl siding installation is now the gold standard for Burlington exterior renovations because it solves all three of these problems simultaneously at a fraction of the maintenance cost.

What Does Vinyl Siding Cost in Burlington?

For a standard detached home in Burlington (approximately 1,800 sq ft), vinyl siding installation typically ranges from $8,000 to $14,000 fully installed. Premium insulated styles with higher R-values run closer to $16,000 for complete homes.

  • Standard vinyl panels: $4.50–$7.00 per sq ft installed
  • Insulated vinyl panels: $7.00–$10.00 per sq ft installed
  • Removal of old siding: adds $1,200–$2,500 depending on layers

Choosing the Right Thickness

Not all vinyl is created equal. Bargain siding is typically 0.040 inches thick — thin enough to crack in severe Ontario ice storms. We exclusively install panels at 0.044 inches or thicker, which provides genuine structural rigidity and a noticeably superior finished appearance.

Signs Your Burlington Home Needs New Siding

  • Bubbling, warping, or cracking panels along south-facing walls
  • Visible mould or discoloration starting from the bottom courses
  • Ice dam buildup at roof edges pointing to inadequate insulation underneath
  • Higher-than-normal heating and cooling bills

FAQ

How long does vinyl siding last in Ontario's climate?

Premium vinyl siding, properly installed with correct ventilation, routinely achieves 30–40 year lifespans in Ontario with virtually zero maintenance beyond annual washing.

Do you offer colour matching for existing trim?

Yes. We carry over 60 standard colours and can source specialty colours through our supplier network. We'll bring physical samples to your Burlington home before ordering.
