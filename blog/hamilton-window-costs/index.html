How Much Does Window Replacement Cost in Hamilton (2026 Guide)

Expert Insights • Apex Windows & Exteriors

The Real Cost of Upgrading Hamilton Homes

If you own an older home in the Hamilton area, you likely feel the winter drafts and see the heating bills climbing. Understanding the true cost of window replacement is critical to budgeting heavily for ROI and efficiency.

Average Cost Estimates (2026)

On average, homeowners in Hamilton can expect to pay between $800 to $1,500 per window fully installed, depending on the glass configuration and framing.

  • Standard Double-Hung (Vinyl): $800 - $1,050
  • Large Casement Windows: $1,100 - $1,400
  • Custom Bay/Bow Windows: $2,500+

What Factors Influence Price?

First, the glass material. In Southern Ontario, triple-pane glass is heavily recommended over double-pane to effectively seal out sub-zero snaps, naturally bumping the price by about 15%.

Second, if the underlying wooden frames are rotted (a frequent problem in historical Hamilton districts), custom brick-to-brick installation is required, which slightly extends labour costs compared to simple retrofit inserts.
