Port Dover Lakefront Living: Durability Secrets

By Apex Expert Team | March 12, 2026

Living on the shores of Lake Erie in Port Dover is a dream, but the environment can be harsh on your home. Intense winds and high humidity demand a higher standard of exterior products.

1. High Wind Pressure Ratings

In Port Dover, standard windows often fail during lake storms. It's essential to look for windows with high Design Pressure (DP) ratings. These units are engineered to resist bowing and leaking even under extreme atmospheric pressure.

2. Corrosion-Resistant Hardware

Moisture near the lake can cause standard window and door hardware to rust or pit within just a few years. We recommend stainless steel or specially coated hardware for all Port Dover installations to ensure smooth operation for decades.
